## Getting Started

### Account Types

The platform supports three types of accounts:

- **Customer**: Browse designs, place orders, track deliveries
- **Creator**: Design custom sneakers, manage portfolio, earn from sales
- **Admin**: Platform management and support

### Registration & Login

1. **Visit the Platform**: Navigate to the main website
2. **Choose Your Role**: Select "Customer" or "Creator" during registration
   - **Customer**: Browse designs and order custom sneakers
   - **Creator**: Upload designs and earn from sales
3. **Complete Profile**: Fill in your details and preferences
4. **Verify Email**: Check your email and click the verification link
5. **Start Using**: Begin exploring the platform based on your role

### Profile Setup

**Essential Information:**
- Full name and contact details
- Shipping address
- Payment method
- Notification preferences

**Creator-Specific:**
- Portfolio description
- Design specialties
- Social media links
- Business information

## For Creators

### Understanding the Creator Role

As a creator, you can:
- Design custom sneaker artwork
- Upload and manage designs
- Set visibility and remix permissions
- Earn from design sales
- Track design performance

### Design Creation Process

#### Step 1: Choose a Silhouette
1. Browse available sneaker silhouettes
2. Select a silhouette that fits your design concept
3. Review panel specifications and constraints

#### Step 2: Upload Artwork
1. **Prepare Your Artwork**:
   - High resolution (300+ DPI)
   - PNG or JPG format
   - Maximum file size: 10MB
   - Transparent background (recommended)

2. **Upload Process**:
   - Click "Create New Design"
   - Select your silhouette
   - Upload artwork files
   - Add design title and description

#### Step 3: Design Validation

The platform automatically checks your design through quality gates:

**Gate A - IP Compliance**:
- Text detection (OCR)
- Similarity matching
- Trademark checking
- Shape analysis

**Gate B - Manufacturability**:
- DPI requirements (300+)
- Bleed requirements (0.125")
- Color count limits
- Panel coverage minimums

#### Step 4: Design Management

**Visibility Settings**:
- **Private**: Only you can see
- **Public**: Anyone can browse and order
- **Remix Allowed**: Others can create variations

**Design Status**:
- **Draft**: Work in progress
- **Ready**: Available for orders
- **Archived**: Hidden from public view

### Advanced Design Features

#### Panel Mapping
- Map artwork to specific sneaker panels
- Adjust positioning and scaling
- Preview how design looks on the shoe

#### Design Revisions
- Create multiple versions
- Compare different iterations
- Maintain version history

#### Design Analytics
- View order statistics
- Track earnings
- Monitor design popularity

### Earning from Designs

#### Revenue Model
- Earn commission on each order
- Platform handles payment processing
- Automatic payout system

#### Performance Tracking
- Sales analytics dashboard
- Earnings reports
- Popular design insights

### Common Questions

#### For Creators

**Q: How do I get paid for my designs?**
A: Earnings are automatically calculated and paid monthly. You'll receive payment for all orders placed in the previous month.

**Q: What file formats are supported?**
A: We support PNG and JPG files with a maximum size of 10MB. PNG with transparent backgrounds is recommended.

**Q: Can I edit my designs after they're published?**
A: Yes, you can create new revisions of your designs. Previous orders will use the version that was active when they were placed.

**Q: How do I know if my design passed quality checks?**
A: You'll receive email notifications about design status. Check your design dashboard for detailed feedback.

#### For Customers

**Q: How long does shipping take?**
A: Production typically takes 2-3 weeks, plus shipping time. Total delivery time is usually 3-4 weeks.

**Q: What if my order doesn't fit?**
A: We offer size exchanges within 30 days of delivery. Contact support to arrange an exchange.

**Q: Can I cancel my order?**
A: Orders can be cancelled within 24 hours of placement if they haven't entered production yet.

**Q: What's the return policy?**
A: We offer 30-day returns for quality issues. Contact support to initiate a return.

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

#### Upload Problems
- **File too large**: Compress your image or reduce resolution
- **Invalid format**: Convert to PNG or JPG
- **Upload fails**: Check your internet connection

#### Payment Issues
- **Card declined**: Verify card details and available funds
- **Payment processing**: Wait a few minutes and try again
- **Billing address**: Ensure it matches your card

#### Design Problems
- **Quality gate failed**: Review feedback and make adjustments
- **Preview not loading**: Refresh the page or try again later
- **Design not saving**: Check your internet connection

### Getting Technical Support

If you encounter technical issues:

1. **Check Help Resources**: Look for solutions in the knowledge base
2. **Clear Browser Cache**: Refresh your browser and try again
3. **Try Different Browser**: Test with Chrome or Firefox
4. **Contact Support**: Provide detailed information about the issue
